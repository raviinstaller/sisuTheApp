Hello,
It’s rough out here.

Mental health is the foundation on which you build the life you want, but at times, it can feel just out of reach. Sisu is a trauma-informed toolkit to help you manage anxiety, panic and stress. (And of course, your overall mental health/ illness, if you so identify.) We are your pocket support system that keeps you connected to yourself, your inner circle, and community with a variety of trauma-informed and evidenced-based tools at your fingertips. That means, accessible to everyone, and science approved!

Panic

This toolkit provides information to promote relaxation and release stress in situations where you might not have more than 1-5 minutes. Find your immediate relief here.

Stuck

The stuck resources are for longer term experiences that effect mental health, covering issues like emotion, thought and body management. These tools are meant to be attempted when an individual has longer period of time and should be practiced regularly. If you’re searching for immediate relief, try the panic tool kit.

My Inner Circle

This is a messaging system helps you get in contact with your support system in times of urgent need as well as providing tools to aid a discernment and create supportive connections. Connection is a vital aspect to managing mental health and stress.

What we're about?

Evidence-based

Sisu utilizes evidence-based research and trauma informed methods to regulate stress response, anxiety and panic along with practices to improve mental health care. This means that every piece of information utilized has been vetted by the psychological community for effectiveness, (for those with trauma, and those without).

Trauma-informed

This app focuses on the importance of trauma-informed stress management, such as somatic (body) based nervous system regulation, cognitive strategies such as mindfulness, compassion and acceptance, and relational healing tools. Sisu doesn't center on a singular strategy, but offers you a variety of resources and approaches. As some of you might have noticed personally, practicing only meditation or only thought management hasn't worked for all your stress or every situation. Here at Sisu, we provide you, the unique individual, with a wide array of approaches so that you may find the most fitting exercise for each moment.

Accessibility

Our goal is to increase accessibility to valuable tools for all seeking to manage stress, anxiety, panic, trauma or overall mental health. We know that therapy is a privilege too many can’t afford. Sisu has sourced all of the most effective trauma-informed tools that many therapists use to help support your mental health journey. Sisu seeks to offer these tools to you in one place, with an easy to use interface where you don’t have to commit high levels of your personal data, time or attention to get the help you need. Sisu can also be used in conjunction with seeing a therapist when you need on-the-go support or without. However, we are not meant in any way to replace therapy. It is always recommended to work with a therapist when dealing with trauma or mental distress.

Transparency

Please subscribe to our newsletter for more information on why each psychological tool has been shown to manage anxiety, stress and panic, as well as the biological and psychological mechanisms behind maintaining mental health. The objectives of Sisu are for transparency and the demystification of mental health* because mental health as vital as physical health*
*Mental health as termed here, does not mean the absence of what some regard as mental illness.* The CDC states that mental and physical health are equally important components of overall health.
